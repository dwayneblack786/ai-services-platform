# 30-Day Plan: Build a Steady Income Stream with AI-Driven Productivity

Date: 2026-04-15
Owner: [Your Name]
Primary Goal: Create a repeatable monthly income stream by selling one AI-enabled productivity offer to a focused niche.

## 1) Outcome Targets (Day 30)

Set realistic but meaningful targets:

- Revenue target: $1,000 to $3,000 MRR (1 to 3 paying clients)
- Pipeline target: 20+ qualified conversations
- Sales target: 2 to 5 paid pilots
- Delivery target: 1 standardized service workflow with SOPs and templates
- Time target: 8 to 12 hours/week maximum for service delivery per client

## 2) Offer Strategy (What You Sell First)

Start with a service, not a SaaS product.

Service Name:
- AI Productivity System Setup (Done-for-You)

Core Promise:
- Save clients 5 to 15 hours/week and improve response speed/consistency in one business process.

Possible Use Cases (pick one):
- Lead follow-up automation + weekly pipeline summary
- Meeting notes to tasks + accountability tracker
- Content repurposing pipeline (long-form to social/email)
- Proposal/report drafting workflow with quality checklist
- CRM cleanup + follow-up sequence automation

Delivery Format:
- 7-day setup sprint
- 30-day optimization retainer

Pricing Suggestion:
- Pilot (first 30 days): $500 to $1,200
- Ongoing monthly retainer: $900 to $2,500

## 3) 30-Day Execution Plan

## Week 1: Niche + Offer Validation

Objective:
- Lock one niche, one problem, one offer.

Tasks:
- Choose one niche you understand (real estate agents, consultants, agencies, coaches, local service businesses).
- Interview 5 to 10 target users (15-minute calls).
- Document top 3 recurring workflow bottlenecks.
- Build one-page offer statement:
  - Problem
  - Outcome
  - What is included
  - Timeline
  - Pilot price
- Draft one mini case simulation (before/after time savings).

Outputs by end of Week 1:
- Niche statement (1 sentence)
- Offer page (Google Doc/Notion)
- Discovery call script
- Pilot scope and pricing

## Week 2: Build a Reusable Delivery System

Objective:
- Make delivery fast and repeatable.

Tasks:
- Build your production stack:
  - AI drafting: ChatGPT or Claude
  - Automation: Zapier, Make, or n8n
  - Workspace: Notion or Airtable
  - Comms: Slack/email templates
- Create SOPs for each step:
  - Intake
  - Setup
  - QA review
  - Weekly reporting
- Build template assets:
  - Prompt library
  - Onboarding questionnaire
  - Client weekly update template
  - ROI/time-saved tracker
- Add human QA checks to every automated output.

Outputs by end of Week 2:
- SOP document (v1)
- Automation workflow (v1)
- Client onboarding pack
- Weekly KPI dashboard template

## Week 3: Client Acquisition Sprint

Objective:
- Fill pipeline and close pilots.

Tasks:
- Outreach volume goal: 15 to 25 personalized messages/day.
- Channels: LinkedIn, email, warm network, niche communities.
- Offer a free 3 to 5-minute workflow audit video.
- Use this CTA: 20-minute call + fixed 30-day pilot.
- Run 8 to 15 discovery calls during the week.
- Close 2 to 5 paid pilots.

Messaging Framework:
- Who you help
- Bottleneck you solve
- Quantified outcome
- Low-risk pilot structure

Outputs by end of Week 3:
- Outreach tracker with response rates
- Call notes and objections list
- Signed pilot agreements

## Week 4: Delivery, Proof, and Retainer Conversion

Objective:
- Deliver measurable wins and convert pilots to recurring revenue.

Tasks:
- Onboard pilots within 48 hours of payment.
- Implement one high-impact workflow first.
- Send weekly scorecard:
  - Time saved
  - Throughput increase
  - Follow-up speed improvement
  - Error reduction
- Collect testimonial and permission for anonymized case study.
- Present next 60-day roadmap and convert to monthly retainer.

Outputs by end of Week 4:
- At least 1 documented outcome
- At least 1 testimonial
- Retainer proposal for each pilot
- MRR established

## 4) Weekly KPI Dashboard

Track these numbers every Friday:

- Outreach sent
- Replies received
- Discovery calls booked
- Discovery calls completed
- Pilots closed
- Pilot-to-retainer conversion rate
- Hours saved per client (estimated + verified)
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)

Simple KPI math:

- Reply rate = Replies / Outreach
- Booking rate = Calls booked / Replies
- Close rate = Pilots closed / Calls completed
- Conversion rate = Retainers / Pilots

## 5) Risk Controls (Avoid Common Failure Modes)

Risk: Too broad niche
Mitigation:
- Narrow to one audience and one workflow for 30 days.

Risk: Over-automation with poor quality
Mitigation:
- Keep human QA gate before client delivery.

Risk: Custom work for every client
Mitigation:
- 80 percent standardized, 20 percent tailored.

Risk: Underpricing
Mitigation:
- Price against outcomes (hours saved, speed, consistency), not effort.

Risk: Inconsistent lead flow
Mitigation:
- Protect daily outreach block as non-negotiable.

## 6) Suggested Daily Schedule (60 to 120 minutes/day)

Monday to Friday:

- 20 min: Outreach and follow-ups
- 20 min: Proposal/call prep
- 20 min: Delivery or workflow optimization
- 10 min: KPI updates
- Optional 30 to 50 min: Discovery calls and onboarding

Saturday (optional 60 to 90 min):
- SOP improvements
- Prompt/template refinement
- Case study drafting

## 7) Scripts You Can Use Immediately

Short outreach message:

"I help [niche] remove [specific workflow bottleneck] using a lightweight AI workflow. Typical result is [time saved/outcome] in 2 to 4 weeks. If helpful, I can send a 3-minute teardown video of your current process and where automation can help."

Discovery call opener:

"Walk me through your current process from start to finish. Where do delays, rework, or dropped tasks happen most often?"

Close question:

"If we can improve this workflow in 30 days and show measurable time savings, are you open to a monthly optimization retainer?"

## 8) 60-Day Extension (After Day 30)

Once you have initial MRR:

- Add a second niche offer only after first offer is stable
- Build low-ticket digital assets (templates/SOP kit)
- Start a referral incentive for existing clients
- Consider lightweight SaaS only after repeated demand pattern is clear
