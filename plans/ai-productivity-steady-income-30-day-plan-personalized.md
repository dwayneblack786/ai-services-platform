Objective:

Tasks:

- Use diagnosis script:

  - Current response time?
  - Where do leads get dropped?
  - Estimate-to-close rate?
- Close with low-risk pilot:
  - 30 days
  - One workflow
  - Measurable KPI baseline + delta

Output targets:

- 2 to 4 paid pilots closed
- Signed simple agreement
- Payment before onboarding

## Week 3: Fast Delivery + KPI Baseline

Objective:
- Deliver visible operational win in 7 days.

Tasks:

- Onboard each pilot within 48 hours.
- Deploy the core workflow only.
- Track baseline metrics day 1:
  - Lead response time
  - Leads contacted within 10 minutes
  - Booked estimate count
- Send week 1 scorecard by end of week.

Output targets:

- 2 pilots fully live
- 1 weekly scorecard/client sent
- At least one measurable improvement signal

## Week 4: Proof + Retainer Conversion

Objective:
- Convert pilots into recurring monthly clients.

Tasks:

- Present results in a 20-minute review call.
- Show before/after in business terms:
  - Faster first response
  - More follow-up touches completed
  - More estimates booked
- Propose 60-day optimization plan:
  - Add missed-call text back
  - Add no-show reactivation flow
  - Add monthly reporting cadence

Output targets:

- 50 percent or higher pilot-to-retainer conversion
- 1 to 3 retainers signed
- MRR foundation established
